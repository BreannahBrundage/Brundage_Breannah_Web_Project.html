Safety Measures

Utah is an amazing place to explore beautiful landscape, but there are a few things to keep in mind while exploring Utah. You can check out this page for some important safety measure to consider.

Before taking your trip to Utah: Research & Preparation
First you should research the area you want to see to understand the areas terrain and possible conditions. By doing this research it can also help you plan out the route for safe travels and identifying gas station locations. Planning your route can help choose a beautiful scenic drive to see more along the way.

During Your Trip: Staying Safe in the Climate
It is extremely important to stock up on an appropriate water supply, Utah can have an extremely hot climate in the summer months and it poses a serious health risk for heat related illnesses. Ensure to wear appropriate attire for the hot or cold climate, and of course bring out the sunscreen. Know your limits when exploring especially when visiting the famous National Parks, some areas can be quite challenging and it is best to know when to turn around. There are points of high elevation in Utah and it is important to ensure if you are hiking or driving up to do so at a slow rate to safely acclimate to the elevation change.
Emergency Precautions
Item Description
First Aid Kit Carry a basic first aid kit for minor injuries, this is especially important if you plan on hiking.
Charged CellPhone and GPS Cell service can be spotty, so having a backup paper map could be really helpful in an emergency situation. When you have signal, check in with your emergency contacts so they are aware of your updated location in case you do not return.
Fire and Flashlight In the event of an emergency it is always best to prepare for dark conditions by bringing different light sources. Check for fire advisories as well to prevent starting a wild fire, certain times of the year can be very dry and pose a serious fire risk.
Knife or Multi-tool If you happen to find yourself stuck or tangles up, a knife or multi-tool would be an essential item to need. It is always best to prepare for worst case scenarios even though it may not happen.
